HomeMate in a nutshell...

HomeMate is one of the fastest growing professional cleaning services in Pune today, starting off as just a simple service to connect cleaning vendors to customers, today HomeMate is a household name.

HomeMate realizes the key to our success and total customer satisfaction is consistency. Providing a consistent service ultimately rests with the men and women who are in your facility each day performing the actual cleaning tasks. We provide our employees with the training, proper equipment and supplies they need to perform their duties in the most proficient manner possible. This also means treating those employees with respect and appreciation.

Our success is not solely defined by a set of numbers each month, but rather by how satisfied both our employees and customers are. We believe there is a direct correlation between the two and as a result, every day decisions are based on achieving high marks in both areas.
